About the Data

Sources

Data is compiled from Natural Resources Canada (NRCan), Paren Q4 2025 report, BC Hydro, Hydro-Québec, and other provincial providers.

Charging Levels

Level 1 — Standard 120V outlet, ~5 km/h of charge.
Level 2 — 240V, ~30-40 km/h of charge.
DC Fast — 400V+, ~250-350 km/h of charge.

Methodology

Prices shown are provincial averages for public/commercial charging. Residential rates, subscriptions and member rates are not included.
